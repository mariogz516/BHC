Mission

Mission Statement

The mission of the Black History Committee is to broaden the worldview of the student body at Shrewsbury High School through the analysis of historical events that have shaped American culture.

We seek to recognize the contributions of African Americans to the history of the United States, and to remind students that black history is not just about race - it is about who we are as a nation, where we have come from, and where we are going.

Faculty Advisors: Mrs. Lawlor & Mr. Gonzales

Talent Auditions

2020-2021 | Audition Dates to be determined

The success of each Black History Assembly relies entirely upon YOU. Once the assembly's theme is selected, students are encouraged to develop related creative acts. Auditions are held the week before Thanksgiving break in the auditorium.

Previously selected acts have included dance routines, singing and/or musical performances, spoken word performances, and video creations.
